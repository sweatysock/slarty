Welcome to Audence

An experimental live venue platform for artists.

The artist can perform live and hear their global audience clap and cheer as if they were in a real venue.

Audence works best with Firefox, and best of all on PC or Mac.

Three green lights in the bottom right corner indicate you have a great connection. Congratulations!.

It can take a few minutes for the network and software to get comfortable with each other. If any of the lights are orange for a while the sound may be garbled. Red means there are serious issues and there may be no sound.

If the top light is orange your device does not have enough power to run the software properly. If the other two lights are orange there are problems with your network. Refreshing the page or switching between Firefox and Chrome can sometimes resolve these issues.
